Temples in Barkur

Barkur is home to several important temples that reflect its religious heritage. The town is known for the Sri Bairagi Ganapathi Temple, an ancient and revered temple that attracts pilgrims from all over. The temple is famous for its unique architecture, with beautiful stone carvings and sculptures that date back to the Chola period.

Other notable temples in Barkur include the Sri Vishnu Temple and the Sri Durga Parameshwari Temple, which are significant sites of worship and have historical significance within the local community.
