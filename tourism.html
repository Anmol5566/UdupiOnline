Tourist Attractions

Udupi Sri Krishna Temple

One of the most revered temples in South India, famous for its distinct idol of Lord Krishna.

Malpe Beach

Famous for its pristine beauty and water sports, Malpe Beach is a must-visit for beach lovers.

St. Mary's Island

Known for its unique geological formation and crystal-clear waters, perfect for boat trips and relaxation.
